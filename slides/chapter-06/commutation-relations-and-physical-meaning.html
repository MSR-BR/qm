Commutation relations and physical meaning
Cyclic commutators encode incompatibility and rotational geometry
3 / 15
Guided reading

The canonical commutators imply that angular-momentum components close under commutation: the commutator of two components is proportional to the third.

Angular-momentum algebra
\[[\hat L_i,\hat L_j]=i\hbar\epsilon_{ijk}\hat L_k\]
\[[\hat L_x,\hat L_y]=i\hbar\hat L_z\]
One explicit derivation

Start with \(L_x=yp_z-zp_y\) and \(L_y=zp_x-xp_z\). Expand the commutator. Terms involving different coordinate-momentum directions vanish; the survivors are \(i\hbar xp_y-i\hbar yp_x=i\hbar L_z\).

Cyclic companions
\[[\hat L_y,\hat L_z]=i\hbar\hat L_x,\qquad [\hat L_z,\hat L_x]=i\hbar\hat L_y\]

Reverse the order of either commutator to reverse its sign.

Physical consequence

Nonzero commutators mean no common basis can diagonalize two distinct components. Preparing a state with definite Lz generally produces a distribution of results for Lx and Ly.

Uncertainty preview
\[\Delta L_x\Delta L_y\ge\frac{\hbar}{2}|\langle L_z\rangle|\]

A large measured z projection forces a nonzero joint transverse spread. The full covariance form is developed later.

Do not overinterpret

The relation does not say that components lack reality or that measurement devices are imperfect. It says the quantum state itself cannot assign arbitrarily narrow distributions to incompatible components at once.

Worked reasoning sequence

Use the following chain when working with Commutation relations and physical meaning. First identify the symmetry, prepared state and observable being discussed; this determines which basis and quantum numbers are meaningful. Next write the operator or differential equation before substituting eigenvalues. Apply boundary conditions, commutators or normalization one step at a time, keeping dimensions and allowed quantum-number ranges visible. Only then simplify to the highlighted result. Finally translate the mathematics into a measurement statement: specify which outcomes are possible, which quantities remain uncertain, how degeneracy is counted, or where probability is concentrated. This sequence prevents a correct formula from becoming disconnected from the physical assumptions that justify it.

Algebra-to-physics audit

Before accepting an angular-momentum calculation, verify four layers. Operator layer: preserve order and use the correct commutator sign. Spectrum layer: confirm that m changes only in unit steps and remains between -l and +l. Representation layer: state the basis order before reading matrix rows or columns, and check Hermiticity for observables. Physics layer: distinguish an exact eigenvalue from an expectation value or variance. A zero transverse mean does not imply a zero transverse measurement; a basis rotation does not change the underlying observable; and a semiclassical vector cone does not assign simultaneous sharp values to all three components. These checks connect the formal algebra to what an actual sequence of measurements can reveal.

Algebra predicts experiments

If a beam is filtered by one angular component and then measured along another axis, it generally splits again. Matrix noncommutation and measurement disturbance are two descriptions of the same structure.
