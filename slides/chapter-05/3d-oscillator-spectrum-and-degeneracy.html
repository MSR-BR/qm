3D oscillator spectrum and degeneracy
Spherical and Cartesian state counting give the same shell multiplicity
11 / 15
Guided reading

The energy depends only on the total shell number N. In spherical coordinates, different l and m states occupy that shell; their total count must match the number of nonnegative Cartesian triples.

Energy shells
\[E_N=\hbar\omega\left(N+\frac32\right),\qquad N=0,1,2,\ldots\]
Allowed orbital labels
\[l=N,N-2,N-4,\ldots,0\ \text{or}\ 1\]

For each l, \(m=-l,\ldots,l\).

Degeneracy

Counting nonnegative solutions of \(n_x+n_y+n_z=N\), or summing \(2l+1\), gives

\[g_N=\frac{(N+1)(N+2)}{2}\]

A built-in consistency check

If Cartesian and spherical counting disagree, an allowed l or m value has been missed. Both descriptions represent the same energy eigenspace.
